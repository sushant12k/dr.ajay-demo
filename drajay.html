Meet the Doctor

14+ Years of
Trusted Care.

Dr. Ajay Agrawal brings over 14 years of rigorous medical experience to Bilaspur. Practicing at Krishna Skin Clinic in Sarkanda, his foundation is built on elite training from Kasturba Medical College (Mangalore) and JJMMC (Davangere).

Known throughout the region as a humble and careful listener, Dr. Agrawal prioritizes accurate diagnosis and patient education over aggressive interventions. Whether treating complex cases like Vitiligo or managing routine skin health, his approach is always calm, thorough, and highly effective.
